I3A Store

In this website you can register for the events or conferences organized by the institute.

  • Add the corresponding product to the cart, and proceed to payment. The cart only allows one registration in order to collect the data of each participant. Please make one registration per participant.
  • You can use credit card or bank transfer. You can also choose the University of Zaragoza Internal Payment option .
  • Our invoice will be e-mailed to you within 5 workdays after registration. In the name and VAT Nr of your institution, including also the registered person data (name and ID/Passport Nr).
  • For more information you can read our Terms and Conditions of use.

To see more details, click on the image or on the title of the event you want to register for.
